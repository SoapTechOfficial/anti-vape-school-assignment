Vapes are marketed with bright colours and sweet flavours, but many contain nicotine and chemicals that can damage your lungs, affect your brain, and make it harder to focus at school and in sport.

Built as a school assessment to raise awareness and support healthier choices.

Inside a typical vape
Not just “water vapour”. It can include nicotine, metals, and chemicals.
  • Nicotine can change how your brain develops.
  • Flavouring chemicals can irritate lungs.
  • Heavy metals may come from the device coil.
